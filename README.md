## Board use

The study in `Index.html` is intended for review ahead of the upcoming Board meeting. Two specific actions are being requested:

1. Select one of three COLA trigger options as a standing policy.
2. Direct Rudd and Wisdom to include the overtime service credit conversion and a COLA affordability estimate in their next biennial valuation (December 31, 2025).

The data handoff to R&W is scheduled for April 2026.
